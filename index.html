Products

With innovative and sustainable production processes, we produce granulates of the highest quality.

Our Bridgecom regranulate is available with both PIR (post-industrial recycled) and PCR (post-consumer recycled) material.

Our range of services includes plastics from PP, PE to PS, ABS, PP and many more. On request we can produce the material with the desired PCR content. We also offer regranulate with different product specifications (colour, additives).

This allows us to respond to the needs of our customers in the best possible way and to produce the right granulate for a wide range of applications within the plastics industry.
